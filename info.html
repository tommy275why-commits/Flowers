How To Order

Currently, all floral collections on our shop page are customized on demand. To request an arrangement, please click 'Inquire' or reach out via our contact page. Provide your budget, preferred colors, and delivery timelines, and Karson will handle the rest.

Arrangement Care Instructions

Keep your fresh blooms thriving as long as possible with basic clean care:

  • Trim stems at a sharp 45-degree angle before setting them in fresh water.
  • Change the vase water completely every two days to limit standard bacteria build up.
  • Display your arrangement away from harsh, direct sunlight and draft spots.
