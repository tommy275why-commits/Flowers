How it all started

It began with a tiny garden and a big dream. Our founder started growing flowers in her back garden as a hobby — a few packets of seeds, a pair of rusty scissors, and a whole lot of hope.

🏡

Our home studio

We work out of a converted potting shed — yes, really! It's cozy, it's chaotic, and it smells absolutely heavenly. Every bouquet is made by hand right here.

✨

Why we do it

We believe flowers have a superpower: they make people smile. Whether it's a birthday, a thank you, or just a Tuesday that needs brightening up — we love being a part of that magic.
