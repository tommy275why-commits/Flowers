The Botanical Process

It began with a small home garden and a vision for clean, intentional design. Our founder, Karson, started growing and compiling local flowers as a creative outlet, which naturally evolved into a dedicated studio practice focused on premium aesthetics.

The Design Studio

Operating locally out of a private studio, everything is processed, conditioned, and styled by hand. We prioritize absolute quality and structure over high-volume production.

Our Philosophy

We believe floral styling is an art form that transforms interior spaces. Every stem is chosen deliberately to ensure balance, movement, and structural longevity.
